Atlanta Canvasing Trip

On August 24 - 26, 2017, a trip was made to Atlanta by a contingent from Baton Rouge comprised of two residents of EBRPHA properties, board members and staff of the East Baton Rouge Parish Housing Authority, board members and staff of Partners Southeast, and consultant facilitators. This group of twenty persons toured three exemplary mixed-income housing developments produced and managed by Integral Group in coordination with the Atlanta Housing Authority.
